How It Works

Search/Create Posts

Have/Want something to swap? Awesome! Search for what you want or what you are willing to trade. Else, create a post mentioning what you have and what you want.

Connect

Found someone who has what you want or someone has found you? Great! Contact them. Discuss the logistics, exchange pics, etc.

Send/Ask for Invitation Code

An Invitation Code helps ensure a smooth transaction with your Swap Pal by connecting you both in our system.

If you are a post author, go to your Profile Page to send the Invitation Code to your Swap Pal.

If you are not the post author, ask them to send you the Invitation Code.

Swap!

Meet your Swap Pal at the agreed time and location. Swap your items and do a spot check for the item's condition.

Rate your Swap Pal

Was your Swap Pal trustworthy? Did they deliver what they promised? Let us know by rating them. Your comments will be anonymous.
